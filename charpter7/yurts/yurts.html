The Yurts at pacific Trails


What is a yurts?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground.Each yurts is fully enclosed with canvas walls, and a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurts is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quit and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and to dress for changing weather with light for changing weather with layers of clothing.It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
