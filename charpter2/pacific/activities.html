Activities at Pacific Trails

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special loging experience on the california North Coast. Relax in serenity with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean.

Hiking
Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to state park. Go it alone or jion one of our guided hikes.
Kayaking
Occean kayaks are avaliable for guest use.
Bird Watching
While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
