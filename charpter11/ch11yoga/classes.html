Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of pose and show movement focuse on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama(breathwork), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginner are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focus on breath-synchronized movement-- you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, retorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experence. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.

Relax Anytime with Savasana

Prepare yourself for savasana. Lie down on your yoga mat with your arms at your side with palms up. Close your eyes and breathe slowly but deeply. Sink into the map and let your worries slip away. When you are ready, roll on your side and use your arms to push yourself to a stting position with crossed legs. Place your hans in a prayer position. Be grateful for all that you have in life. Namaste.
